Quattro sport rear differential.
With the ability to transfer power to the appropriate wheel while cornering, the available sport quattro rear differential on the Audi RS 6 Avant performance helps you stay in control while powering through a turn.

RS-tuned adaptive air suspension.
Bringing out the precision and thrill of driving, the controlled damping in the air suspension system was given taut tuning specifically to suit the Audi RS 6 Avant performance. Depending on your drive select mode, it can lower or raise your ride height for a more engaging drive.

High-performance V8 engine.
The Audi RS 6 Avant performance has a potent, 4.0L twin-turbo V8 engine, twin-turbocharged with 621 HP propelling it from 0 to 100 km/h in 3.4 seconds.

Aerodynamic design.
Every curve and vent is shaped with purpose, enhancing performance and efficiency.

RS mode.
Mounted within easy reach of the driver's thumb, the RS mode button allows you to save two customizable driving profiles.
