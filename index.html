Driver Assistance



Top view camera system.
Zoom in on your vehicle's exterior with the top view camera system with Virtual 360. Toggle between 3D camera views and swipe to view different angles-all from the comfort of the driver's seat.

Audi active lane assist with Emergency assist.
The Audi active lane assist with Emergency assist monitors the position of the vehicle and helps you stay within lane markings via steering guidance. If you are inactive, the Emergency assist takes control.

Adaptive cruise assist with Traffic Jam assist.
Be less stressed in traffic with adaptive cruise assist with Traffic Jam assist. Maintain a preset distance to the vehicle ahead or even come to a complete stop using throttle, braking, and steering guidance.

Night vision assistant.
With optional Night vision assistant, you're able to see pedestrians or large animals ahead while driving in the dark. The intuitive, thermal imaging camera detects objects and projects images onto the Audi virtual cockpit.

Remote park assist plus.
Remote park assist plus automatically maneuvers the RS 6 Avant performance in and out of a perpendicular or parallel parking space. This enhanced feature can be performed via your phone when you are outside of your vehicle.
